# Week 8 Overview: Projects and AI Ethics

This page is the starting point for Week 8.

## Week Goal

By the end of this week, students should be able to finish a small project more clearly, make practical decisions about
what to prioritize, and reflect on ethics and limitations in a way that fits their own work.

## This Week

This is a support week. It is not a week for adding lots of new technical complexity.

It is a week for:

- Finishing the core workflow.
- Explaining the work clearly.
- Reflecting on limitations and ethics.
- Preparing a final submission that is easier to understand.

A small, complete, well-explained project is usually much stronger than a large unfinished one.

## Completion Checklist

- I can describe my problem and dataset clearly.
- I can tell what is essential to finish before submission.
- I can explain my workflow in a short and understandable way.
- I can describe at least one limitation of my work.
- I can reflect on ethics practically.
- I can prepare my work so that it is clear and easy to follow.
