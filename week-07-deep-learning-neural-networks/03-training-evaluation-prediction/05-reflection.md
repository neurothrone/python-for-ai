## Recall Questions

2. What does `fit(...)` do?
3. What kind of information is stored in `history.history`?
4. What does `evaluate(...)` tell you?
5. Why do sigmoid outputs often need a threshold before they become class labels?
6. What extra evidence would help you compare this network fairly with a simpler baseline?
