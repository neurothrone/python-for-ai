# 02. AI and ML in Simple Words

## Why This Matters

This course is not only about Python syntax. It also helps you understand the ideas behind AI work, where AI is used,
and how machine learning fits into that bigger picture.

## Artificial Intelligence in Simple Words

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is a broad field about building systems that can do tasks that usually need human-like
intelligence.

Examples include:

- Recognizing patterns.
- Making recommendations.
- Classifying text or images.
- Supporting decisions from data.

## Narrow AI and General AI

Two common ideas are:

- **Narrow AI**: built for one specific task or a small set of tasks.
- **General AI**: a theoretical system that could handle many different intellectual tasks at a human level.

> [!NOTE]
> Most AI used today is **Narrow AI**.

Examples of Narrow AI:

- A music app recommending songs.
- A system that sorts images into categories.
- Spam filtering in email.

## Machine Learning in Simple Words

Machine learning, or ML, is a subset of AI. In simple terms, ML means learning patterns from data so the system can make
a prediction or decision later.

That means:

- All machine learning is part of AI.
- Not all AI is machine learning.

## Three Common ML Styles

- **Supervised learning**: learns from labeled examples, such as input-output pairs.
- **Unsupervised learning**: looks for structure or patterns without labels.
- **Reinforcement learning**: learns from rewards and penalties over time.

At this stage, the main thing to remember is that ML uses data to learn patterns.

## Where AI Is Used

AI is used in many everyday areas, for example:

- Recommendation systems in streaming and shopping apps.
- Support tools in healthcare.
- Forecasting in business and logistics.

## Key Idea to Keep

AI is the big field. Machine learning is one way to build AI systems. Most AI examples in everyday life are Narrow AI.
