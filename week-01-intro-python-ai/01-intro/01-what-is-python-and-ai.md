## What is Python

Python is a programming language.

It is designed to be readable and beginner-friendly, while still powerful enough for advanced software and AI systems.

## Why Python is used in AI

Python is common in AI because it has:

- Clear, readable syntax.
- A large ecosystem of AI and data libraries.
- Strong community support and learning resources.
- Good tooling for experiments and production.

Common AI libraries include `pandas`, `scikit-learn`, `tensorflow`, and `keras`.

## Real-World AI Examples

- Recommendation systems in streaming and shopping apps.
- Spam filtering in email.
- Image and text classification.
- Forecasting for business and logistics.
